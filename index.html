Rentbot

Rentbot is a simple solution that tries to auto parse your email-bills and then send out en email to every romm-mate at the end of the month.

It is currently still in development & needs a lot of improvement

Example topology

<rentbot>

<bill name="rent" fixed="2000.0">
    <user name="John" ratio="0.55"/>
    <user name="Adam" ratio="0.45" />
</bill>

<bill name="cox" from_email="Do_Not_Reply_Prod@cox.com">
    <user name="John" ratio="0.50"/>
    <user name="Adam" ratio="0.50" />
</bill>

<bill name="sdge" from_email="do-not-reply@sdge.com">
    <user name="John" ratio="0.50"/>
    <user name="Adam" ratio="0.50" />
</bill>


<bill name="erenterplan" from_email="support@leasingdesk.com">
    <user name="John" ratio="0.50"/>
    <user name="Adam" ratio="0.50" />
</bill>

</rentbot>

Explanation

Each bill has a name & either a 'fixed' tag or a 'from_email' tag.

  • fixed: use the actual value
  • from_email: looks through emails received from this specific host & try to extract the $ amount.

Each bill also contains different people that owe money to that payment of this bill. This is called a user. They are simply defined by their name & how much of the bill that need to pay (as a ratio)

Required Permissions

If you do not want this program to read your email then feel free to use the static portions or extend a file handler class. Your information is safely stored on your computer using secure AES encryption along with a bcrypt salted hash. You will require a password to enter the program, so pick an intelligent password.
